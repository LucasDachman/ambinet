Throw off PIEs

Personal Information Extractors can identify you through facial recognition, voice recognition, and behavior fingerprints. They build a profile on you and serve you ads on the streets. Our AI is configured to block these trackers.

Where you go says a lot about you

We don’t associate your location with your identity. Personal information such as saved locations and routes stay on your BioChip and are not broadcasted to PIEs.

What you say should not be public

AI conversations are kept secure on your BioChip and data is only broadcasted anonymously for the purpose of improving our services. All outgoing voice audio is stripped of identifying attributes through the use of audio distortion algorithms.

Keep Your Financials Private

What you buy, where you bought it, and how much you paid is sensitive information. We don’t store, sell, or use that information. Your spending history never leaves your BioChip.

Your Body Is Nobody’s Business

From your heart rate to your reproductive cycle, apps for your health can give you insight into some of your most personal details. We let you take control of what information your BioChip collects, doesn’t collect, and who has access to it.
